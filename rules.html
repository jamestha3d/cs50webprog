Here are some rules to know about the game

Pawn Promotion

If a pawn reaches the other end of the Board it may be promoted to any piece of choice.

En Passant

Usually the pawn can move only one step, however for each pawn, its first move may be two steps.
Note: if a pawn moves two steps as its first step, a nearby enemy pawn, who could have captured it, if it had moved only one step, can still capture it.(even though the moved pawn is now side by side with the enemy pawn) this is called capturing en passant. This move must be done immediately.

Who plays first

The white piece always plays first.

Check

When the King finds itself in the capture path of any piece, that is to say, the King is in position where a piece can capture it. the king is said to be under check, and the next move is to remove the king from danger.
There are only three ways a king can get out of check:
  • move out of the way (though he cannot castle!)
  • block the check with another piece
  • or capture the piece threatening the king.

Castling
